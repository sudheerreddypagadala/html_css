AB de Villiers: The Who Redefined Modern Day Cricket

AB de Villiers, also known as Mr. 360, revolutionized modern cricket with his unparalleled 360-degree batting prowess. His innovative strokeplay, lightning-fast reflexes, and fearless approach redefined what was possible on the field. He left audiences awestruck with his ability to score runs in any direction with power and precision.

Records and Statistics

  • Fastest ODI century (164 runs off 31 balls) - 2015 World Cup vs West Indies
  • Fastest Test century (100 runs off 32 balls) - 2015 vs Pakistan
  • Most centuries in international cricket (43) - across all formats (Tests, ODIs, T20Is)
  • Highest score in IPL (176 runs off 38 balls) - 2016 for Royal Challengers Bangalore

Beyond Batting

While primarily known for his batting brilliance, AB de Villiers was also a capable wicket-keeper and exceptional fielder. He donned the wicket-keeping gloves for South Africa in limited-overs formats for several years, known for his athleticism and sharp reflexes behind the stumps. He also displayed outstanding fielding skills throughout his career, taking impressive catches across various positions.
